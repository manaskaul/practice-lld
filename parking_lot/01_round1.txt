USE CASES (System Behavior)
    Vehicle enters the parking lot
    Vehicle gets the ticket
    Vehicle parks in the designated spot (floor number and spot)
    Vehicle gives back the ticket and pays
    Vehicle leaves the parking lot

ENTITIES
    ParkingLot
    Vehicle (Car/Truck/Bike)
    TicketCounter
    ParkingSpot
    Floor
    Ticket
    PaymentCounter

RESPONSIBLITIES
    ParkingLot
        for managing overall system of vehicle parking
    Vehicle
        the actual vehicle that will get parked
    TicketCounter
        the counter that issues the ticket to the vehicle that enters the lot
        it assigns the floor number and the parking space where the vehicle can be parked
    ParkingSpot
        physical spot where the vehicle can be parked
        1 parking spot can only have 1 vehicle parked in
    Floor
        physical floor (or floor number) where the vehicle can be parked
        it will have multiple parking spots
    Ticket
        physical ticket containg the vehicle details and parking details
        vehicle uses this to find and park their vehicle at the correct spot
    PaymentCounter
        the counter that accepts the payment and lets the vehicle exit
