Requirements :

1. Building will have multiple floors
2. Building will have multiple elevators (2 for starters)
3. Elevator will serve all the floors 0 to N
4. Elevator moves up/down and can stop on any floor
5. Every floor will have a panel to call the elevator, with only 2 options - up or down to generate elevator request
6. Every elevator will have option to enter the destination floor once person enters
7. Elevator will have the knowledge of which floor it is on, which direction it is moving, and floors assigned to it

System Events:

User clicks the elevator call button on the floor
An elevator is assigned to the call floor
The assigned elevator could be idle or one moving the call direction
The elevator reaches the call floor
User gets on the elevator and presses the destination floor in the cabin
Elevator serve the floor one-by-one and moves to the floors in the direction
After serving all the floors in one direction, elevator reverses the direction and service the other floors



User-driven events (external)
    User presses UP or DOWN button on a floor
        A call request is generated
    User presses a destination floor button inside an elevator
        A destination floor request is generated

System-driven events (state observations)
    An elevator arrives at a floor
    Elevator doors open
    Elevator doors close
    An elevator has no pending destination floors

Time / progression events (completion of actions)
    Elevator completes movement between two floors
    Elevator finishes servicing a stop
